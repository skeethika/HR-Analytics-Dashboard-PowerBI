##Process

Requirement Gathering
Identified HR business requirements and the KPIs to be analyzed.
Data Collection
Collected employee data containing demographics, salary, performance, attrition, hiring, and training details.
Data Cleaning & Transformation
Cleaned missing values, corrected data types, removed duplicates, and transformed data using Power Query.
Data Modeling
Created relationships between tables and structured the data model for analysis.
DAX Calculations
Created measures and KPIs such as Total Employees, Attrition Rate, Average Salary, Active Employees, and Performance Rating using DAX.
Dashboard Design
Designed interactive dashboards with KPI cards, bar charts, pie charts, line charts, maps, and slicers.
Analysis & Insights
Analyzed workforce trends, employee attrition, hiring patterns, salary distribution, performance, and employee satisfaction.
Business Recommendations
Provided insights to help HR improve employee retention, hiring strategies, training programs, and workforce planning.

##Project Insights

The organization has approximately 13K employees with an attrition rate of 11.6%, indicating opportunities to improve employee retention.
Sales and Corporate Functions experience the highest employee attrition, requiring focused retention strategies.
Employees aged 26–35 years and those at Job Level L1 have the highest turnover.
The average monthly salary is around 6.34K, while senior job levels receive significantly higher compensation.
Overall job satisfaction and work-life balance are moderate, suggesting scope to enhance employee engagement.
Hiring, training, and promotion trends provide valuable insights for workforce planning and talent development.

##Conclusion

The HR Analytics Dashboard successfully transforms HR data into meaningful business insights. By monitoring key metrics such as employee attrition, hiring, salary, performance, and satisfaction, the dashboard enables HR managers to make informed, data-driven decisions. It supports better workforce planning, improves employee retention strategies, and enhances overall organizational performance through interactive and real-time reporting.
